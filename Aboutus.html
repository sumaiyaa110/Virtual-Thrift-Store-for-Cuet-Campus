About CUET Thrift Store

By Students, For Students

What Is CUET Thrift Store?

CUET Thrift Store is a student-driven initiative at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET) that enables students to buy and sell second-hand items easily, affordably, and sustainably.

Our Purpose

We know student life can be tough on the wallet. That’s why we created a platform where students can exchange books, electronics, clothing, and dorm essentials—at student-friendly prices.

What Makes Us Special?

  • Built by CUETians, for CUETians
  • Promote sharing, reuse, and affordability
  • Reduce waste and support a sustainable campus
